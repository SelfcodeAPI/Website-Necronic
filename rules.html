Follow the rules

List of all Minecraft and Discord server rules.

Ignorance of the rules is no excuse!

General rules of conduct

  1. Respect other players and their opinions. No insults, bullying, discrimination or harassment.
  2. Avoid disruptive or excessive spamming in chat.
  3. Follow the instructions of the server staff. They are responsible for the smooth functioning of the server.
  4. Do not use cheats, hacks or modifications that give you an unfair advantage.
  5. Avoid deliberate griefing or stealing from other players.

Building rules

  1. Do not build offensive, obscene, or racist structures.
  2. Avoid excessive redstone placement so as not to impact server performance.

Trade and Economy

  1. Trade between players is allowed and encouraged. However, take care to trade fairly and avoid cheating.
  2. Exploiting game bugs or glitches to gain unfair wealth is prohibited.

Violations of the rules

  1. Rules violations may result in warnings, temporary bans or permanent bans, depending on the severity of the violation.
  2. If you have any disagreements or questions about a rule violation, contact the server staff. Discussions about violations should be private and respectful.

If you have read the rules, you can go and enjoy the fun on our server!
